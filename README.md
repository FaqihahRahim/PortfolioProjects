Overview
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The dataset has information of orders made at Olist Store from 2016 to 2018. I analyzed 97K+ orders from the Olist public dataset to understand the delivery performance and fulfillment efficiency across the supply chain. 
I selected only the tables relevant to my experiance, focusing on order processing efficiency, fulfillment reliability, and delivery performance. I intentionally excluded product-level tables to keep the analysis aligned with operational KPIs.

II) Key Insights:

1) What is the total revenue generated by Olist, and how has it changed over time?

   Answer: I aggregated the sum of the payment value column by the year. From our Sum of revenue,  Olist generated a total of $15.6M across 3 years. We can see a there was a consistent rise in total revenue earned by Olist over time. 

<img width="874" height="502" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8f59fb17-9528-46f7-ad13-518eea9b78d3" />

2) How many orders were placed on Olist, and how does this vary by month or season?

   Answer: The total number of orders placed on Olist was 97,404. In November 2017, Olist received the highest number of orders, with approximately 7,351 orders. The lowest number of orders occurred in December 2016, with only one order.

3) What % of orders status?

   Answer: The order status of Olist Store is for delivery rate is 99.05% with cancellation and processing are less than 1% each. This shows that Olist is strong in operations performance despite high demand orders during peak periods.
